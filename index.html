About us

Driving change in the textile industry

CremeXchange is a group of companies tackling textile waste through recycling and upcycling, driving landfill waste diversion and sustainable innovation. Operating across the textile lifecycle, we sort and grade textiles at CremeXchange Togo and retail upcycled fashion through Worn Again Ghana. Leveraging proprietary software and an online marketplace, we ensure full traceability and transparency at every stage. Our mission is to build a circular economy by diverting 100% of textile waste in Ghana by 2035, creating 100,000 green jobs by 2040, and reducing carbon emissions by 30% by 2035. Through advanced technology, global partnerships, and ethical practices, we are empowering communities and inspiring a greener, more equitable future.
